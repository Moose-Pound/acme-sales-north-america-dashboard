ACME Sales North America Dashboard

Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes sales performance across North America using the ACME dataset. The objective of this project was to uncover meaningful insights, understand sales patterns, and support data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard was developed by applying practical skills gained during the Alison – Mastering Power BI certification, including data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, and effective dashboard design.

Business Problem

Many organizations struggle to clearly understand their sales performance, particularly when dealing with large datasets. This includes identifying top-performing products, understanding regional performance, and tracking revenue trends over time.This dashboard was designed to address these challenges by providing a clear, interactive, and insightful view of sales data.

Key Insights

-	The United States contributes the majority of total revenue (approximately 78%), indicating strong market dominance.
-	A small group of products, including Crosser, Optimizer, and Warper, generate most of the revenue.
- Revenue is concentrated in key regions such as USA02 and USA07.
-	Sales show an overall upward trend, with noticeable fluctuations across different periods.
-	Some products and regions underperform, highlighting potential areas for improvement.

Recommendations

-	Expand business strategies in underperforming regions to balance revenue distribution.
-	Invest more in top-performing products to maximize profitability.
-	Improve marketing and pricing strategies for low-performing products.
-	Analyze peak sales periods and replicate successful strategies.
